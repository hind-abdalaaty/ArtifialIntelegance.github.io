What do you mean by artificial intelligence?

Artificial intelligence (AI) is an area of computer science that emphasizes the creation of intelligent machines

that work and react like humans. Some of the activities computers with artificial intelligence are designed for include:

Speech recognition. Learning. Planning

From the start I say that we need to define intelligence and define what we mean by human intelligence.

The stage of intelligence applies to any situation seeking to solve complex problems.

According to the Oxford dictionary definition, intelligence is the ability to understand, learn and think.

learning or understanding from experience, extracting meaning from a matter that is likely to mean
